Progress Update (March 2020)

We are working on developing the model for recognizing smoke emissions using the labeled videos. The following examples show promising results. The heatmap (reddish areas) on top of the images show the locations where the model thinks have smoke emissions.

Currently, the model did not make good use of the temporal information (e.g., smoke fades away slower than steam) in the video clips. We are working on improving its performance. Once the model is stable, we will work on a visualization of smoke events that are predicted by the model.
